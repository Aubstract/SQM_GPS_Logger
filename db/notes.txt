Data file columns:
- trigger_id: unique identifier for the trigger
- measurement_id: unique identifier for the measurement
- time_utc: timestamp in UTC
- time_local: timestamp in local time
- latitude: latitude of the measurement location
- longitude: longitude of the measurement location
- altitude: altitude of the measurement location
- speed: speed of the measurement
- satellites: number of satellites used in the measurement
- gps_time: time it took to get the GPS information
- sqm_time: time it took to get the SQM information
- temperature: internal temperature of the SQM
- count: number of internal measurements
- frequency: frequency of the SQM
- period: period of the SQM
- brightness: brightness level measured by the SQM
